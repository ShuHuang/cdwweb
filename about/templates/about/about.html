About ChemDataWriter

ChemDataWriter is a transformer-based toolkit for auto-generating books that summarise research.

Features

Reader processes the raw XML/HTML paper files and converts them into a Reader object.

Finder suggests the key topics of the paper corpus.

Retriever retrieves the papers from the corpus based on the key topics.

Summariser summarises the papers based on the input text.

Paraphraser paraphrases the summarised text.

Title Generator generates the short title in terms of the original long title.

LaTeX Generator reorganises the summarised text and converts it into a LaTeX file.
